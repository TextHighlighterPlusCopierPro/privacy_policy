Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Text Highlighter Plus Copier Pro ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension. This extension is developed and maintained by Pranay Patel.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects the following types of information:

  • Text selections and highlights you create on web pages
  • Your highlight color preferences and settings
  • Basic usage statistics to improve the extension

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the highlighting functionality
  • Save your preferences and highlighted text
  • Improve the extension's features and performance

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using the browser's storage system. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Your data is stored locally and is not accessible to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not use any third-party services or analytics tools.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through the extension's interface
